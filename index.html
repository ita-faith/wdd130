Nigeria

Nigeria is a West African Country, , the most populous on the continent, with over 200 million people. It is a diverse nation with more than 250 ethnic groups, the largest being the Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. Nigeria has a rich cultural heritage, reflected in its vibrant arts, music, and literature scenes. The country is a major player in Africa’s economy, largely due to its vast oil reserves. Its capital is Abuja, though Lagos is the largest city and a key financial hub. Despite its economic potential, Nigeria faces challenges like corruption, poverty, and political instability.
